CLUBS

Citizen Consumer Club

Citizen Consumer Club is one of a kind, it creates awareness about consumer rights & obligations of companies to meet societal expectations on quality and safety. Join us to embark on a journey of creating & gaining awareness on this unique & much needed topic.

Villupaatu Club

Villupaatu is also known as Villadichampattu. It is an ancient form of musical storytelling in India in which narration is interspersed with music. It is an art of southern states of Tamil Nadu, Kerala and North-East Sri Lanka. In this club, we encourage students to perform villupaatu for various events.

Wellness Club

Wellness Club focuses on physical, emotional & mental wellness aspects of our students. Yoga classes conducted by external resources & internal trained students, Martial arts classes, etc are a few examples of activities that happen in the club. Join us to know more.

Art Club

Three core tenets of artistic expressions are Iyal, Isai, Nadagam, and music sits right in the middle of it. We learn to jam, train on vocals, play a new instrument, join the choir and much more. Our college also teaches us to play traditional instruments like Veena, apart from the usual guitar & keyboard. Join us & sing your heart out.

Dance Club

We are the highlight of any college function. Be it traditional dances like Bharatanatyam or Western Classical or Kollywood beats we bring life to the event and believe in spreading joy with some amazing moves! Over numerous practice sessions, while getting fit, we also get to bond with one another! Join us to find more.

Drama Club

Picking on from Music club’s description, Nadagam or drama is an age old medium of storytelling. Drama brings out your narrative skills, artistic talents and is truly a way to win people’s minds and hearts. Many great actors, politicians and activists have used this powerful medium to convey their ideas. Join us & tap in to your hidden talents.

Eco Conservation Club

For students who want to spread awareness on the importance of co-existence & sustainability of our nature & its resources. If you have a green thumb and love the outdoors, join our club for a greener tomorrow.

National Service Scheme Club

The National Service Scheme (NSS) is an Indian government-sponsored flagship for public service program conducted by the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports of the Government of India. Popularly known as NSS, the scheme was launched in Gandhiji’s Centenary year in 1969. Aimed at developing student’s personality through community service, NSS is a voluntary association of young people in Colleges, Universities and at +2 level working for a campus-community linkage.

CELLS

A formal entity, with student participation in addressing specific areas of student development & needs.

Placement Cell

With an increasing number of companies & startups focusing on keeping up with the growth market trend, & more students graduating every year, there is both demand & competition in the job market. A dedicated Training and Placement Cell ensures our graduates stay ahead of the competition and are capable to meet market’s skill demands. Lead by two full time placement officers and student representatives from each department acting as place coordinators, the placement cell functions around the year, focussed on the below areas.

  • Conducting placement training program for students.
  • Inviting leading organizations for campus recruitment.
  • Disseminating information, event schedules & Organizing a well planned recruitment drive.

    • Entrepreneurship Development Cell

    Entrepreneurship Development Cell (EDC) intends to motivate the students to view entrepreneurship as a desirable career option in the early years after graduation. EDC provides financial assistance, arranges interaction with successful entrepreneurs and creates opportunities for skill building and capacity enhancement.

  • To educate enterprising students on the essentials of starting a business
  • To provide an exhaustive resource pool to aid potential student entrepreneurs
  • To develop personal attributes and skills that form the basis of entrepreneurial mind set and behaviour

    • Idea & Innovation Cell

    Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD), Govt. of India has established ‘MHRD’s Innovation Cell (MIC)’ to systematically foster the culture of Innovation amongst all Higher Education Institutions (HEIs). The primary mandate of MIC is to encourage, inspire and nurture young students by supporting them to work with new ideas and transform them into prototypes while they are in their formative years. MIC has envisioned encouraging creation of ‘Institution’s Innovation Council (IICs)’ across selected HEIs. A network of these IICs will be established to promote innovation in the Institution through multitudinous modes leading to an innovation promotion.

    EVE Cell

    The EVE- Empowerment through Values and Ethics Cell takes forward the vision and mission of the college by organizing lectures and events that empower the women students to overcome the challenges of life. EVE, the Women’s Cell of our college aims at empowering students by creating awareness of their rights and strength as women and thereby helps them in achieving equal participation in the social, cultural and economic arenas of life. The cell takes forward the vision and mission of the college by organizing several guest lectures by distinguished women achievers and also conducts activities to foster unity, confidence and self-reliance among students.

    Research Cell

    Research is an integral part of Higher Education system. Teaching and Research feed each other and travel in parallel lines for effective education. Thus, it is required that research culture is inculcated at the early stage of learning of students. The CTTE Research Cell provides effective support system in establishing Research-intensive units where students can experience Invention/Innovation-centric learning rather than Information-centric learning.

  • To promote the culture of inter-disciplinary research among faculty and students
  • To inculcate research culture among faculty and students, thereby leading to publishing of papers in referred journals

    • Career Guidance Cell

    The Career Guidance Cell of the College provides necessary career information to students by organising different career guidance programmes through Industry – Institute Convergence and Expert Interaction Sessions to enable students to explore their career opportunities in a particular sector. Career Guidance cell also provides guidance session on Higher Education such as CA, Fashion Designing, Cosmetology etc. Eminent resource persons from various sectors and esteemed institutions are invited for providing training to prepare students to meet their future challenges in terms of career challenges.
