IQAC

Internal Quality Assessment Cell - Aims, Objectives & Functions of CTTEWC IQAC

PROFILE

Internal Quality Assurance Cell is an effective and efficient internal coordinating and monitoring mechanism...

AIMS & OBJECTIVES

  • To generate good practices and ideas.
  • Plan, implement and measure the outcome of academic and administrative performance of the institution.

FUNCTIONS OF IQAC

  • To develop realistic and attainable quality benchmarks for each of the academic and administrative activities.
  • To fix standards for all activities on the basis of its own activities and the experience of others.
  • To keep the institution abreast of and abuzz with quality sustenance activities on a wide gamut of pertinent issues through workshops, seminars, discussions, etc.
  • To strengthen the moral fiber of the institution leading to holistic development.
  • To promote awareness in the institution and be proactive.
