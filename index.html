Be part of our history

Chevalier T Thomas Elizabeth College For Women was established by Dr. Elizabeth Thomas, the wife of late Chevalier T. Thomas - Founder of CTTE trust, with the sole aim of empowering through quality
education and life skill training. Today we have grown manifold & are proud to be a Women's college with unparalleled digital infrastructure and other Educational facilities.

Welcome to CTTE

We are an institution focused on empowering women by providing them high quality education, skills and values to uphold for life. With over 2,300 students from diverse backgrounds pursuing a range of disciplines, CTTEWC is one of the prominent colleges serving Northern Chennai. In addition to our College for women, CTTE Public Trust also manages several schools and continues to spread the light of education to far reaching places and people.
