MARYLAND LIBRARY

-A State of the Art Library facility at CTTEWC.

The present state of art ‘Maryland library‘ in Dr. Elizabeth Thomas Block was inaugurated on 16th June 2016 by Mr.R. Venkatesan, I.A.S and has a collection of more than 27219 documents comprising of books, theses, audio-visual materials, bound back volumes of journals & magazines to meet user needs.The total area of the Library building is about 5206 sq.ft. It accommodates Stack, Periodicals, OPAC (4 systems), PG & Research Section (1 System), Circulation (3 Systems), E-Resource Centre (11 Systems) and Reprography. 5 cameras are placed at strategic points in the library for surveillance and 4 Fire Extinguishers are provided.

AUDITORIUM & CONFERENCE HALL

-Air-conditioned auditoriums & teaching facilities.

T.Thomas Auditorium: A centrally air-conditioned auditorium with a capacity of around 500 is part of our infrastructure. The Auditorium hosts various academic and cultural events of the college. The auditorium is equipped with good acoustics. Justice J.Kanakaraj Auditorium: The new auditorium with centralized air-conditioning and a mezzanine floor was inaugurated by our Managing Trustee and Correspondent, Thiru. L. Palamalai I.A.S.(Retd) on November 23,2017. It has a total carpet area of 3800 square feet and can comfortably seat 700 people.The auditorium has been equipped with an LCD projector also.

CLASSROOMS

-Specialised lab for language studies.

A major hurdle for the students is the English language. The college addresses this issue with the help of a language lab with 31 computers which have been enabled with Renet software which provides an interactive platform for improving study skills, soft skills, English grammar and English pronunciation. Each student is given the computer for 3-4 hours per month on a rotation basis for the first two years of all Under Graduate Course. This exposure to computers also helps students who do not have computers at home to become familiar with the use of computers.
