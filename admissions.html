Apply to Chevalier T Thomas Elizabeth College for Women

CTTEWC was founded with the aim to empower all women through quality education and remains true to this day to its founding goals. If you would like to study in a college in the heart of the city that focuses on changing the world for a better tomorrow, you are choosing the right place. We look at every single applicant’s application, academic milestones and personal interests, and select students who will find our college the best fit for their aspirations.

Rules & Regulations

Our Institution has issued a few guidelines on what is expected out of our students in terms of attire, conduct, academics, and general discipline.

Bachelor’s and Master’s

Sponsorships

Scholarships

The Application Process

Our college facilitates both online & offline applications, read further to know more.

Application Document Checklist

Before you begin the application, please ensure you have the following documents with you. Scanned copy for Online, Photocopy for Offline applications

Online Application Process

The Online application is provided solely for the applicant's convenience. It does not influence the outcome. Kindly read through before applying

1: Gather Necessary Documents

Applications that are incomplete or have incorrect/wrong information or lacking in required documents will be rejected.

For any clarifications, contact the college office – Ph: 044 2537 5753

2: Start Online Submission

Click the “Apply online” button to start the online application process.

Before you begin, make sure you have the digital copy of checklist documents with you, and a reliable internet connection.

3: Submit the Form

Applicants are advised to read the form carefully and provide the mandatory details.

Preview the form and check if the details you entered are correct before submitting.

After filling the form online.

4: Confirmation of Submission

Both the student & guardian will receive notification SMS and email confirming the receipt of the online application.

Note: This is confirmation of application received, NOT confirmation of admission.

5: Shortlisting & Call for Interview

After processing the application, the applicant will be shortlisted by the respective departments applied for.

Information regarding applicants called for interview for all programmes will be sent by e-mail/SMS/Phone calls.

6: Interview & Final Decision

Applicants shortlisted for the interview should bring original documents & complete the interview process.

Prescribed Fees can be paid (online/offline) for the selected course. In order to complete the admission process, original certificates with photocopies should be submitted on the date and time specified.
