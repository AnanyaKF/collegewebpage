CTTEWC’s History

Established in the year 1985, under the T.Thomas Educational Trust, Chevalier, T.Thomas Elizabeth College is a pioneer in self-financing education. It is an exclusive college for women in Perambur – Chennai. The institution believes in empowering women through its holistic education. The Chairman of this prestigious institution is Honourable Justice Thiru. B. Gokuldas, the Managing Trustee & Correspondent is Mr. L. Palamalai, IAS (Retd). The College has completed 38 years of dedicated service in the cause of higher education & has well experienced,committed faculty.

Accreditation Status

Chevalier T.Thomas Elizabeth College for Women is a grade 'A' NAAC accredited institution.

The college was accredited by NAAC with CGPA of 3.04 on four point scale at A grade.

The accreditation is valid for 5 years and re-accreditation will be in 2024.
